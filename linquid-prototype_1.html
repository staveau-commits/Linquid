QuID Score System
5 Levels of Identity Trust
Each level reflects a richer, more verified identity profile — unlocking more merchant intelligence and more consumer rewards.
Q1

Basic Identity

Basic information provided at enrollment. Entry-level identity for simple interactions.

Enrollment
Q2

Linked Source

At least one link to another source of data — a social account, device, or email provider.

Linked
Q3

Multi-Linked

Multiple links to other sources. Required for standard merchant fast-checkout integrations.

Verified
Q4

KYC-Linked

At least one link to a property requiring Know Your Customer verification — e.g. a bank account.

KYC
Q5

Government ID

At least one government-issued reference provided. Maximum trust, highest-value transaction access.
