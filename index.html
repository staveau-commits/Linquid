About LinqUID

The Identity Layer
Commerce Is Missing

Every person carries five distinct identity layers — but has no way to control which is exposed, when, or to whom. LinqUID bridges this gap: a portable, consented commerce identity that works across channels, merchants, and AI agents.

As agentic commerce accelerates, the need for a verified, sovereign identity credential becomes urgent. LinqUID is that credential — built on social identity, secured by consent, and designed for both human and AI-driven commerce.
